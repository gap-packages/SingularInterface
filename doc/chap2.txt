  [1X2.1-3 [33X[0;0YExample[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YHere  is  a  short  example  in  [5XSingular[105X  4.0.1  demonstrating  some  basic
  procedures.  [5XSingular[105X uses [10X=[110X for assignments and suppresses any output while
  [5XGAP[105X  uses  [10X:=[110X  for assignments and triggers the so-called [10XView[110X-method, which

  trailing  [10X;;[110X).  Basically,  [5XSingular[105X's  print  procedure  is  mapped  to the

  [33X[0;0YStart by loading [5XSingularInterface[105X in [5XGAP[105X.[133X

    [4X[28Xtrue[128X[104X
  [4X[32X[104X
  
  [33X[0;0YDefine  the  ring  [23XR  :=  \mathbb{Q}[x_0,  x_1, x_2, x_3][123X (with the monomial
  ordering [10Xdegrevlex[110X):[133X
